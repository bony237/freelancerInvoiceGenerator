What is an invoice?

An invoice is a list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due for these; a bill. It is the detailed summary of services rendered and the cost for the services. It can be seen as a record of transaction between a customer and the seller.

Why do you need an invoice?

As someone that renders services, you need to be able to have a document that clearly states the service rendered and the equivalent cost because without this, you do not have an evidence of what you have done and you cannot keep track of pending payments from customers. Invoice, is therefore very important.

Creating an Invoice

To create an invoice using the invoice Generator is super easy. There is a given set of guidelines to follow in order to have a seamless flow while using the application. To do this, you need to fill out the form with the necessary details.

  1. Numbering of invoices

    2. This is a basic numbering of the invoices to avoid mix-up and confusion. For instance, if your invoice spans multiple pages, the numbering allows you to know the order in which the invoices can be displayed

  2. Invoice date, Due date

    3. The invoice date is the date in which the invoice was created and presented to the customer. The due date is the date in which the payment is supposed to be made to you.

  3. Services and Goods Information

    4. This is a breakdown of the services rendered and the description of the services with their specified prices or cost based on the rate and quantity given to the customer.

  4. Price, payment type

    5. Total price of services rendered and the method of payment should be added to the invoice. Method of payment can be via transfer, cash and so on.

  5. Customer details

    6. This refers to the customer’s name, address, business name, email, phone number. This enables you to be able to keep track of your customers using their details.

  6. Invoice creator’s details

    7. This includes your contact information: your name, address email, phone number, billing address and so on.

Exporting your invoice

When generating your invoice, there is a button that tells you to export your invoice. If you are a new user, once you click on the button, it automatically redirects you to the sign up page to register your account. This procedure is to guarantee safe keeping of your invoice, in case it is needed for other purposes.

Viewing all invoices

As a user, once you are logged in, you can view your previous invoices by accessing the View Invoices on the sidebar. This is where you can view all the previous invoices, export them if you want to. This is very good as you know that your invoices are stored and can be used later on.
